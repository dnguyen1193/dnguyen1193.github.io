Hello, world!

I'm a 2016 Tufts University graduate with a bachelor's in computer science. I currently work at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard, a non-profit genomic research center, as a full stack engineer on the Genotype Tissue Expresion (GTEx) Portal. The GTEx project aims to provide the scientific community with a resource to study the effects of genetic variants on human gene expression.

Currently, I'm interested in learning how to create interactive visualizations and/or doing more front-end work. I'm hoping to spend time on my own learning d3 and experiment with different JavaScript frameworks.
